Welcome to SCI Virtual Dashboard for Equipment Management and Inventory

This dashboard simplifies the process of finding and reserving equipment and rooms within the School of Computing and Information.

Background

The School of Information Sciences, the Department of Computer Science, and the Intelligent Systems Program joined forces to establish the School of Computing and Information (SCI) in 2017. This merger aimed to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation within the field of computing and information. However, as the SCI continued to evolve, the challenges of managing equipment and room reservations became apparent. Currently, the SCI operates with two separate legacy systems, which pose inefficiencies in equipment and room management. Navigating the campus and locating specific buildings, such as Sennott Square, the Information Science building, and Bellefield, can also be a challenge for students, faculty, and staff. The need for a cohesive and user-friendly solution to address these issues is clear. The proposed project, the "SCI Virtual Dashboard for Equipment Management and Inventory," is designed to bridge these gaps and provide a streamlined, efficient solution for equipment and room reservations, ultimately enhancing the overall user experience within the SCI department.
